1. Overview

EngageRank is a Chrome extension that analyzes publicly available Instagram profile data to calculate engagement metrics. This privacy policy explains what data the extension accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Data We Access

EngageRank accesses the following data from Instagram, only when you actively initiate an analysis:

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your Instagram credentials or passwords
  • We do not access your direct messages, stories, or private content
  • We do notstrong> access private account data (unless you already follow them)
  • We do not store any data on external servers
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or telemetry services
  • We do notstrong> share any data with third parties
  • We do not collect your browsing history

4. How Data Is Processed

All data processing occurs entirely within your browser:

  • Data is fetched directly from Instagram's public APIs using your existing browser session
  • Engagement calculations and content analysis run locally using JavaScript
  • Cached results are stored in browser memory and cleared when you close the tab
  • No data is sent to any server owned or operated by EngageRank

6. Instagram Terms

EngageRank accesses only publicly available data through Instagram web interface. Users are responsible for complying with Instagram Terms of Service when using this extension. EngageRank is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Instagram or Meta Platforms, Inc.

7. Children Privacy

EngageRank is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
